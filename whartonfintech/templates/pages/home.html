Careers

Connecting exceptional business and engineering talent with FinTech companies and investors globally

We at Wharton FinTech are continuously building our network of industry contacts, including innovative companies, investors and academic centers, to deliver on our dual missions of establishing ourselves as thought leaders in the sector and sourcing internship and full-time employment opportunities for students.

We have successfully sourced opportunities at leading companies such as Wealthfront, NerdWallet, Apple Pay, Personal Capital, and MasterCard, in addition to building relationships with accelerators and investors such as FinTech Collective, Citi Ventures, Barclays and Techstars.

If you're interested in learning more or in advertising a career opportunity to our member base, please contact us at careers@whartonfintech.org.
